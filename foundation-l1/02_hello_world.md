## Understanding `print()` ğŸ“

### What does `print()` do?
`print()` is like telling the computer: "Show this on the screen!"

Think of it like:
- **You:** "Hey computer, show this message!"
- **Computer:** "Okay!" *displays the message*

### The Rules of `print()`
1. **Always use parentheses:** `print()`
2. **Put your message in quotes:** `"Hello, World!"`
3. **Don't forget the exclamation mark!** (optional, but fun!)
